What are CS2 ingame commends?

In Counter-Strike 2, in-game commends are positive feedback given by players to other players for exhibiting good behavior, such as being friendly, helpful, or demonstrating leadership qualities during a match. Commends are a way to show appreciation for good conduct, as well as to promote a positive and respectful gaming environment.

Commends are displayed on a player's profile, and can help to improve their reputation in the Counter-Strike 2 community. They are also considered a positive factor in CS:2 matchmaking system, which can lead to better matches with other skilled and respectful players.
